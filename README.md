# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce retail dataset using SQL to uncover valuable business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and geographic trends.
---

## Business Problem

The company has accumulated a large volume of transaction data but lacks clear visibility into:

* Overall sales performance
* Revenue-generating products
* High-value customers
* Geographic sales distribution
* Seasonal sales patterns
* Customer purchasing behavior

Without proper analysis, management may struggle to identify growth opportunities, allocate resources effectively, and develop targeted customer retention strategies.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset:** Online Retail Dataset

The dataset contains over 500,000 retail transactions from a UK-based online retailer and includes:

* Invoice Number
* Product Description
* Quantity Purchased
* Invoice Date
* Unit Price
* Customer ID
* Country

## Key Insights

### Revenue Concentration

A relatively small number of products account for a significant share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of maintaining inventory availability and prioritizing these products in marketing campaigns.

### Customer Concentration

A small percentage of customers contribute a large proportion of total sales, making customer retention a critical business priority.

### Geographic Performance

Sales are concentrated in a few countries, providing opportunities to strengthen high-performing markets while exploring expansion opportunities in emerging regions.

### Seasonal Demand

Revenue peaks during specific periods of the year, particularly around holiday seasons, indicating the need for proactive inventory planning and marketing activities.

### Customer Segmentation Opportunities

Customers exhibit different spending patterns, creating opportunities for personalized marketing, loyalty programs, and targeted promotions.

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Customer Retention

* Implement loyalty and rewards programs
* Offer personalized promotions to high-value customers
* Use customer segmentation for targeted marketing campaigns

### Increase Revenue

* Promote top-performing products
* Introduce product bundles and cross-selling strategies

### Optimize Inventory Management

* Maintain stock availability for best-selling products
* Forecast demand using historical sales trends

### Strengthen Geographic Strategy

* Increase marketing investment in high-performing regions
* Evaluate growth opportunities in underperforming markets

### Enhance Data Quality

* Improve customer identification processes
* Reduce missing customer information to enable deeper customer analytics
